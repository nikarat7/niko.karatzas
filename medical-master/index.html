Presentation

Dr. Dowling is one of the national and international reference in minimally invasive Spinal Surgery. With extensive experience and even more abroad, in countries such as South Africa, Spain, Switzerland, France, Colombia, Germany, South Korea and the United States. Also it emphasizes its contribution to the academic world, being author of textbooks abroad and giving lectures at national and international level.

He's currently heading the United Nations medical and Surgeon team at Spain. Dr. Dowling is a member of the Chilean Medical Association (RCM: 16730-4) and the Chilean Society of Orthopedics and Traumatology, as well as active member of international societies such as SICCMI, IITs and NASS.
